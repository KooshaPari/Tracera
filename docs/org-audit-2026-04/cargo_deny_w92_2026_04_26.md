- **rustls-webpki / bollard cluster** (KDesktopVirt): RUSTSEC-2026-0049/0098/0099/0104 — needs bollard upgrade off rustls 0.22.
- **Schema/lockfile blockers**: Tracely (deny.toml schema), GDK (lockfile corrupt), Sidekick/Eidolon (broken phenotype-shared path) — must repair before they can be audited.
